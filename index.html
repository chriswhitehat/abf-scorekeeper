Scoring notes

The team with the lower selected-player handicap total receives the handicap difference as bonus points in each of the 5 rounds. Each round is worth 2 points to the higher grand total, or 1-1 for a tied round. BBCP is 1 point for the higher match grand total, or 0.5-0.5 if tied. Final points are round points plus BBCP. Break indicators: Home breaks round 1, Visitor round 2, Home round 3, Visitor round 4. Round 5 starts with Home on player row 1 and alternates down the rows.
